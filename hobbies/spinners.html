Welcome to the Spinners APA recreational pool team!

The team plays in an APA 8-ball league on Tuesday nights at 7:30pm and are based out of Lucky Shot in Sunnyvale

Team Objectives:

  • Provide a relaxed environment where people can escape the stress of the valley
  • Provide an opportunity for people to improve their pool game
  • Provide the opportunity for people to compete as part of a team but without significant pressure

My objectives as team captain:

  • Find the best fit of team members expectations for what the team should be
  • Be mindful of peoples time constraints and other obligations
  • Provide team metrics that help people understand and improve their game
  • Be open to suggestions and willing to change the process to provide the best experience for the players

Fees:

  • $15 fee per match played
  • $25 APA membership fee (annual)
  • $5 fee when the team forfeits a spot in a match
